Breakfast Recipes


These breakfast foods are my secret to looking super Batmansy.

The Hash Browns are my personal favorite, deep fried and dusted with pepper jack and mozzarella,
drizzled with spicy dipping sauce and caramelized onions buried inside.

The Avocado Toast has avocado spread, queso fresco, pickled onions, sriracha aioli, and soft poached eggs
all smothered over pan-toasted bread.

The Dutch Pancakes, topped with fresh berries and whipped cream, and the Cinnamon Rolls, with a dollop of
brown butter frosting serve as the perfect sugar rush so I can get on with a day of saving Gotham City from various evildoers.
